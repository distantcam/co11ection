Collected Notes shines when it's used on a mobile device. There's a lot of content that doesn't fit on a tweet, but it's smaller than a full blown post.

iPhone

Our app is written in Swift and leverages a lot of functionality that's only available to truly native-first experiences. No web-app-hybrid oddities or slowness.
